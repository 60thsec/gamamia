GAMAMIA

A weekly collection of fantastic games from new developers and indie favorites.

How it works
⬇

First we look through every nook and cranny of the game development world to find the best new games and then we play them (Our favorite part).

We then meet in a secret cave and decidde on the ones we think you would love.

Finally we put the very best together and send them right to your email in time for you to play them all weekend (Your favorite part).

The Whos and Whats

Gamamia (Gahma-mia) is more than an awesome game newsletter, we're currently working on making Gamamia a web app that will be curated by the community. It's a big project so if you have any comments or feedback that could help. Please let us know here. If you're inerested in joining us, check us out on Assembly.
