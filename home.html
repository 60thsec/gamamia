Submit a Game

We're always looking for awesome games, so if you know of any or if you're developing on yourself please send it our way. We only ask that the game

  • Be playable (a demo works!)
  • Be totally rad

If your game is both of these then just fill out the form below and send it our way! We'll shoot you an email as soon as we play it
